TEDxTheMastersSchool

In my TEDx talk, I provide my analysis and understanding of how diversity is a necessity for the tech industry. Beginning with the current state of diversity in tech, I also talk about how companies are facing issues building products accessible for all customers due to a lack of different perspectives in product design (as well as subsequent scandals in tech companies).

WeClean

WeClean is an app focused on collecting user-sourced data on public trash to propose laws for curbing pollution in certain areas. Prototyped in the Lua coding language, WeClean is a winner of the 2016 ESA LOFT Innovation Fellowship, which gave me the opportunity to speak with officials in federal agencies as well as lawmakers in Congress to see how the app and its algorithms could be used to aid in combatting pollution.
